How Can you know that you are having Mental Health Issues?

Common Signs of having Mental Issues are:

    -Rapidly shifting mood swings -Physical symptoms like headaches -Changes in behaviour

Mental health is very important because when a person is in stress there will physically and mentally getting weaker and weaker that can affect their life and their family's life as well.
