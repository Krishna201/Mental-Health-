How Can you know that you are having Mental Health Issues?

Common Signs of having Mental Issues are:

  • Rapidly shifting mood swings
  • Physical symptoms like headaches
  • Changes in behaviour

Your Mental Health Matters

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Taking care of your mind can improve your wellbeing and quality of life.

About Mental Health

Mental health affects how people think, feel, and act. Common issues include anxiety, depression, and stress.

Ways to Get Help

  • Talk to a trusted friend or family member.
  • Speak with a school counsellor.
  • Contact a mental health professional.
  • Use support websites and helplines.
