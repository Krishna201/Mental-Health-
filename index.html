How Can you know that you are having Mental Health Issues?

Common Signs of having Mental Issues are:

  • Rapidly shifting mood swings
  • Physical symptoms like headaches
  • Changes in behaviour

About Mental Health

Mental health affects how people think, feel, and act. Common issues include anxiety, depression, and stress.
